About Pygskin

Pygskin is a college football analysis project that uses machine learning and data science techniques to reveal interesting trends and information about the sport we love. Scott Ratchford created the Pygskin project during the 2023 FBS postseason and began posting his findings online soon afterwards.

About Scott

Scott Ratchford is a software developer based in the United States. He graduated from the University of Alabama with his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2024. He is finishing his Master's in Business Administration there in 2025. Scott is a full-stack developer with a passion for college football. He combined his interests to make this website and showcase his talents. You can visit his website by clicking here or clicking "by Scott Ratchford" in the navigation bar.
